# FanLoom

FanLoom is a desktop CRM and operations platform for OnlyFans agencies, creators, managers, and chat teams. It brings creator account access, fan conversations, sales reporting, growth tools, employee workflows, billing, and account settings into one desktop application.

Instead of switching between spreadsheets, browser tabs, team chats, and separate reporting tools, FanLoom gives teams a single workspace for daily creator operations. Agencies can link creator accounts, open the OnlyFans experience inside FanLoom, track fan and creator activity, manage employees and roles, and use growth workflows built around real OFM operations.

## What FanLoom Offers

### Centralized creator operations

- Add and manage creator profiles, notes, documents, and linked OnlyFans sessions.
- Open OnlyFans pages directly from the app through an embedded desktop browser view.
- Keep creator-specific sessions isolated so agencies can move between linked creators more easily.
- Relink or remove creator connections when account access changes.

### OF Hub

FanLoom provides a native gateway into common OnlyFans work areas:

- Home and new post workflows
- Notifications
- Chats
- Collections
- Vault
- Queue
- Statements
- Statistics
- Profile and settings

The embedded view is enhanced with FanLoom context, including selected creator state, theme synchronization, fan details, chat detection, and sensitive-word safeguards.

### Message Loom

Message Loom is the dedicated messaging workspace for fan engagement and sales activity. It supports:

- Fan lists, online fans, notifications, and smart-tag filters
- One-to-one fan conversations
- Fan notes and personal details
- Pinned messages and pinned fan lists
- Scheduled messages
- Mass messages and smart mass messages
- PPV timeline and spending activity
- Gallery and vault media browsing
- Favorite emojis, scripts, media attachments, and forwarding
- Fan actions such as mute, restrict, hide, block, and report

This helps chatters work faster while keeping fan context close to the conversation.

### Growth tools

FanLoom includes growth workflows for discovering, segmenting, and converting fans:

- Tracking links with fan-level performance details
- Scripts and script tags for repeatable messaging
- Auto-follow campaigns and campaign details
- Smart tags and smart-tag fan lists
- Free trial links with sources, tags, and collections
- Profile promotions
- Sensitive words management, including import, export, and templates

These tools help agencies test acquisition channels, organize fan segments, and standardize outreach.

### Metrics and reporting

The app includes dashboards and reports for creator, chatter, and fan performance:

- Creator earnings overview
- Subscription, tips, PPV, vault, and total earnings reporting
- Creator performance and chargeback reporting
- Chatter sales, message count, fans chatted, and Loom ratio
- Fan reporting and active fan views
- Date range filters, creator filters, gross/net views, and export support where available

Managers can use these reports to understand which creators, employees, channels, and fan groups are driving revenue.

### Team management

FanLoom supports agency team operations:

- Employee creation and management
- Role-based access control
- Permission management by feature
- Employee shift management
- Creator assignment to shifts
- Chatter selection and reporting
- Password reset and status management

This gives agencies a structured way to delegate work without giving every user the same level of access.

### Billing and account settings

FanLoom also includes account and business settings:

- Billing details and invoices
- Pricing plan views
- Stripe payment verification flows
- Profile updates and email changes
- Password changes
- Sales settings and history
- Theme selection
- Terms, privacy, and about pages

## How FanLoom Helps

FanLoom helps agencies operate with less friction:

- **Less context switching**: teams can manage creator work, fan chats, growth campaigns, and reports from one app.
- **Better fan context**: chatters can see fan notes, subscription history, PPV activity, pinned items, media, and smart-tag context while messaging.
- **Cleaner team accountability**: managers can track chatter sales, messages sent, fans chatted, shifts, and role permissions.
- **More repeatable growth**: tracking links, free trial links, smart tags, scripts, and profile promotions make campaigns easier to run and compare.
- **Safer daily operations**: sensitive-word tools, isolated creator sessions, access roles, and relink workflows reduce operational mistakes.
- **Faster performance reviews**: dashboards and reports surface creator earnings, employee activity, and fan metrics without manual reporting.

## Main App Areas

- **Dashboard**: daily and period-based overview of creator earnings, chatter performance, sales graphs, active fans, and assigned shifts.
- **OF Hub**: embedded OnlyFans workspace for creator account activity.
- **Metrics**: creator reports, chatter reports, and fan reports.
- **Growth Hub**: tracking links, scripts, auto-follow, smart tags, free trial links, profile promotions, and sensitive words.
- **Creators**: creator list, creator creation, profile updates, documents, notes, and OnlyFans connection management.
- **Employees**: employee list, employee creation, role assignment, status management, and shift calendar.
- **Profile Settings**: account profile, role permissions, billing, sales settings, app information, and password management.

## Requirements

- A FanLoom account
- The FanLoom desktop app installed on a supported computer
- Linked creator accounts for OnlyFans-powered workflows
